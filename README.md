

Controls:

  - Move Left & Right: A & D
  - Jump: Spacebar
  - Double Jump: Press Spacebar after Jumping
  - Duck: Hold S while on the ground
  - Dive: Press S while falling
  - Dash: Press LCtrl
  - Sprint: Hold LShift
